Type of Attack and Required Protection Value

· Global: 1
· Targeted: 2

In global or un-targeted attacks (value 1), attackers indiscriminately target as many devices,
services, or users as possible.
They do not care about who the victim is, as there will be a number of machines or services with
vulnerabilities.

Targeted attacks refer to a type of threat in which threat actors actively pursue and compromise a
target entity’s infrastructure.
Typically, these threat actors have a certain level of expertise and have sufficient resources to
